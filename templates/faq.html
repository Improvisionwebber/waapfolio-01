Store Management

Waapfolio is a social commerce platform. It helps you build a simple online store and sell on social media. Here is the flow:
  • Build your storefront in a few minutes
  • Add products with photos, a short description, and a price
  • Share your store link on WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok
  • Get orders in your DMs and manage them from your dashboard
You do not need any tech skill. Just your products and your time.
From your dashboard:
  • Click Add Product
  • Upload product images (up to 5 per product)
  • Type the product name, description, and price
  • Set the stock quantity and product variants (size, color)
  • Click Publish to make it live
Your product will show on your store page right away.

Security & Privacy

Your safety matters. We use:
  • 256-bit SSL encryption: All data is locked while it moves
  • Secure servers: Strong cloud infrastructure
  • GDPR-aligned: We follow global data protection rules
  • No data selling: We never sell your information
If you think your account is not safe:
  • Change your password right away
  • Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Check your recent account activity
  • Contact our support team fast

Troubleshooting

Try these quick steps:
  • Clear your browser cache (press Ctrl + Shift + R)
  • Open the store on a different browser or device
  • Check that your store status is Published
  • Make sure all required fields on your products are filled
  • Contact support if it still does not work
Payments can fail because of:
  • Not enough funds in the account
  • Bank security block (call your bank)
  • Expired card or payment method
Try a different payment method or talk to your bank.
