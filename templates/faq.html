Account & Registration

Creating a Waapfolio account is quick and easy! Simply click the "Register" button on our homepage and follow these steps:
  • Enter your email address and create a secure password
  • Verify your email through the confirmation link we send
  • Complete your profile with your store details
  • Start adding products and selling!
The entire process takes less than 2 minutes.
Yes! Waapfolio supports multiple registration options for your convenience:
  • Google Sign-In: One-click registration using your Google account
  • Email Registration: Traditional signup with email and password
Both methods give you full access to all Waapfolio features.
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a secure link to create a new password. The link expires in 24 hours for security.

Store Management

Waapfolio is a social commerce platform that helps you create and grow your online store. Here's how it works:
  • Create your personalized storefront in minutes
  • Add products with photos, descriptions, and prices
  • Share your unique store link on WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and more
  • Receive orders directly and manage everything from one dashboard
No technical skills needed – just your products and creativity!
Adding products is simple! From your dashboard:
  • Click "Add Product" button
  • Upload product images (up to 5 per product)
  • Enter product name, description, and price
  • Set inventory quantity and variations (size, color, etc.)
  • Click "Publish" to make it live
Your products appear instantly on your store page!
Absolutely! Waapfolio offers various customization options:
  • Store themes: Choose from multiple professional templates
  • Colors: Customize your brand colors and accent tones
  • Logo: Upload your own logo or brand image
  • Layout: Arrange product categories and featured items
Make your store uniquely yours!
Waapfolio includes built-in inventory management:
  • Stock tracking: Set quantities for each product
  • Low stock alerts: Get notified when items are running low
  • Auto-update: Stock automatically decreases with each order
  • Out of stock: Products hide automatically when sold out

Troubleshooting

Try these troubleshooting steps:
  • Clear browser cache: Refresh with Ctrl+Shift+R
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Check if your store is set to "Published"
  • Ensure all required fields are completed
  • Contact support if the issue persists
Payment issues can occur due to:
  • Insufficient funds or card limits
  • Incorrect card details
  • Bank security blocks (contact your bank)
  • Expired payment method
Try a different payment method or contact your bank for assistance.
