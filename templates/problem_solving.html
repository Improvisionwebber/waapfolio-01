The Real Problems Creators Face

Disconnected Sales Channels

Instagram, WhatsApp, TikTok, and payment processors operate in isolation. No unified order pipeline, no shared customer record, no source of truth.

No Native Path from DM to Checkout

Engagement happens on social. Checkout happens somewhere else. That handoff is where most intent-to-purchase silently dies.
