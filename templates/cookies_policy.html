1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites remember your preferences, login info, and usage patterns.

2. How Waapfolio Uses Cookies

  • To remember your login session and preferences.
  • To improve website functionality and performance.
  • To analyze traffic and usage patterns for better services.
  • To personalize content and offers based on your activity.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from third-party services (like analytics providers) to help us understand user activity and improve Waapfolio.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some features of Waapfolio may not work properly if cookies are disabled.
